About Sky Fly

Sky Fly is developing autonomous aerial platforms optimized for emergency healthcare. Our engineering team focuses on safety, redundancy and regulatory compliance while our clinical partners validate medical workflows for in‑flight care and rapid handover.

  • Safety-first design: redundant flight systems, certified batteries and continuous health monitoring.
  • Regulatory-ready: early engagement with aviation authorities and medical regulators.
  • Operational focus: integration with EMS dispatch and hospital logistics systems.
